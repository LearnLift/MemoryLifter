Learning Options

MemoryLifter provides many options for you to customize your learning session.

To customize your learning options, select Tools, Learning options... or click .

 

The following Learning options screen opens:

 

 

Below are the tabs with its various options on the Learning Options dialogue box.

 

Teacher tab

 

You can define how your answers should be assessed. Depending on which teacher type you choose, settings in the other tabs (below) are changed automatically – you do not have to change any settings in the other tabs.  Try each teacher style to see; you can reset back to Default.

 

Learning mode tab

 

Direction

Click the option to have MemoryLifter either ask question and answer as normal, flip answer and question, ask in a random (mixed) basis by flipping answer and question from time to time.

 

Learning mode

MemoryLifter offers five possibilities to learn:

  • Standard mode shows the question on the left side and you have to enter the answer on the right side. MemoryLifter presents you with all available information (i.e., image, sound, example sentences, etc...) for the question.
  • Multiple choice shows you three possible answers and you will have to choose the correct one.
  • Sentences asks you for the sentences instead of the word.
  • Listening comprehension reads the word to you, and you have to enter the answer.
  • Image recognition shows an image and you have to enter the answer.

 

If you check more than one mode, MemoryLifter will choose a random one from the checked ones every time a new card is displayed.

 

Note: If it's not possible to display the card in the selected learning mode (no image, audio, sentence, or not enough other cards), the standard learning mode will be used.

 

Synonyms tab

 

In case a card has more than one meaning (synonym), you can adjust how the program reacts depending on how many of the meanings you know.

  • Promote when 100% known - Only puts the card in the next box when you know all synonyms. Otherwise, the card is demoted to box 1.
  • Promote when >= 50% known - Promotes the card when you know at least half of the synonyms.
  • Promote when first word known - Promotes the card only when you know the very first synonym. Especially useful when you put the most common meaning first.
  • Promote when one word known - Promotes the card when you know anyone of the synonyms. Especially useful when you put the most common meaning first.
  • Prompt when not 100% - When not all translations are known, prompt user.

 

Typing tab

 

Typing mistakes

Select how MemoryLifter should promote/demote cards depending on your typing mistakes.  This is used in conjunction with the "Correct on the fly" option.

  • Only promote when none - Card will be put in the next box if there is no typing mistake at all.
  • Promote when < 50% - Promotes when more than half of the word is written correctly.
  • Always promote - Doesn't care for typing mistakes.
  • Prompt - Prompt user to promote when there have been typing mistakes.

 

Correct on the fly

Click this box if you want your input to be corrected on the fly, that is, you can only enter correct characters, otherwise, the input will stay at the same place.

 

Case sensitivity

Check this if you want your input to be checked for case.

 

Self assessment

When checked, MemoryLifter shows the correct answer and the user can then choose to promote or demote the card based on whether they know it or not.

 

Ignore these characters before/after word

Enter here common punctuation characters that you don't want to be corrected. This gives you more freedom in that you do not have to enter punctuation character like a period or question mark in your answer to get it correct.

 

Snooze/Quit planner tab

 

You can choose to snooze or quit the application after a certain amount of time / cards / correct answers. By clicking one or more of these options, the program will minimize itself after a session, and will stay there.

And it automatically pops up again in the specified time frame. Optionally, the program can quit totally after the session.

 

General tab

Countdown timer

Answer against the clock. The faster you answer correctly, the more points you get.

Display statistics

Have MemoryLifter show the session statistics after each learning session.

Display images

If available, image will be shown on the question.

Auto-play sounds

If available, sounds will be automatically played upon displaying the question/answer.

Play commentary sounds

Check to hear commentary sounds.

Skip showing correct answers

MemoryLifter will proceed right away to the next question after entering the word – correct answers will not be shown.

Random pool

If checked, new words from the pool are taken randomly. Otherwise, words are asked in the same order they were entered.

Confirm Demote

If checked, you have to confirm before cards are demoted.
