Skipping cards you already  know

You can tell MemoryLifter to skip asking cards you already know.

 

To skip asking the current card

 

  1. From the Cards menu, select Maintain.

  2. Check the Don't ask this card box.

 

To skip asking a specific card

 

  1. From the Cards menu, select Maintain.

  2. Select the desired card in the lower part of the window.

  3. Check the Don't ask this card box.
