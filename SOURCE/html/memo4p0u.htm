Maintain

Modifying cards

You can modify, organize, and find cards in a dictionary by using the Maintain card dialog box.  By default, the card currently displayed will be the card shown for editing.

 

Opening the Maintain cards dialog box

From the Cards menu, select Maintain or click on the toolbar.

 

The dialog box is divided into two sections: the upper part contains card data for the currently selected card, and the lower part shows all the available cards in the dictionary.

 

Restricting shown cards

Select either "all cards" or a specific chapter from the Show combo box.

 

Sorting shown cards

You can sort the cards displayed in ascending or descending order by clicking on the appropriate column headings.  For example, to see the displayed cards based on the boxes they are in, click the Box column heading.

 

Selecting cards

  1. To select a single card, click the desired card in the lower part of the window.
    Note: The contents is shown in the upper part of the window.

  2. To select multiple cards, use the standard Windows Shift-click method.
    Note: The only operation you can perform on multiple cards is to delete.

 

Changing a card

1.Select the desired card in the lower part of the window.

2.Make appropriate modifications in the upper part of the window.

3.Click Change to apply.

 

Inserting a new card

  1. Click .

  2. The fields in the upper part of the window become blank.

  3. Add the new card information.

  4. Click Add card when done.

 

Deleting cards

  1. Select one or more cards.

  2. Click .

Warning: This option cannot be undone!

 

Adding an image, audio, or video

You can add multimedia files to the card.

 

Preventing a card from being asked

  1. Select the desired card in the lower part of the window.

  2. Check the Don't ask this card box.

 

Entering special characters

If you need to enter special characters, press the character map button.
