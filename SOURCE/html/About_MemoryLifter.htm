About MemoryLifter

MemoryLifter™ is a powerful memory aids tool for memorization. With MemoryLifter, you can learn a language, improve your vocabulary, become an expert in your field or just remember certain facts. MemoryLifter uses a scientific learning technique (Leitner's box system) to improve the memorization process for better retention in short-term and long-term memory.  It repeats the information shortly before it slips out of your mind and focuses on the information you still need to learn and not what you already know.  In this way, you remember the facts much faster.  MemoryLifter is designed for everyone, including students, parents, teachers, and professionals.

 

With the MemoryLifter software, you simply put in the information you would like to learn in a dictionary of virtual flashcards.  The program takes over the management of the cards and keeps track of your learning progress.  You can create your own cards with text, sound, pictures, and videos or use the pre-made dictionaries like the "English to Spanish" flashcards available for free on the dictionary download section of the MemoryLifter website.

 

Summary of MemoryLifter's major program benefits are:

 

  1. Automated Learning - Automate your learning with Sebastian Leitner's learning system based on flashcards in boxes that was designed to repeat facts to be learned in a specific sequence, depending on how well you remember the facts.

  2. Multimedia Enhanced - Greatly enhance your learning experience by including images, audio and video in the learning files.

  3. Personalized Learning - Create your own flash cards learning files for specific topics you want and include your own multimedia clips to enhance understanding and retention.

  4. Customizable Learning Session - Customize the learning mode you find most useful to match the learning material - standard text input, multiple choice, listening comprehension, self assessment and random presentation.

  5. Measure Your Success - Statistics provide the feedback you need to understand how you learn.

  6. Guaranteed Learning - Use the automated learning planner to regularly quiz you to help you remember the learned knowledge
