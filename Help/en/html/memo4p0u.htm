Maintain Cards

Editing cards

You can modify, organize, and find cards in a Learning Module by using the Maintain Cards dialog box.  

By default, the card currently displayed will be the card shown for editing.

 

To open the Maintain Cards dialog box

Within the Edit menu, click Maintain Cards..., press F10, or click on the toolbar.

 

The dialog box is divided into two sections:
 

  • the upper part contains data for the currently selected card
     

  • the lower part shows all the available cards in the Learning Module.

 

In the upper part you can edit one card at a time, i.e. add or remove information.

In the lower part, called Learning Module overview, you can organize the cards in your Learning Module, i.e. search, delete, or move one card or a selected number of cards to another chapter or box.

 

To prevent a card from being asked

  1. Select the card in the lower part of the window.

  2. Clear the Card is active check box.
    Note:
    If you clear the Card is active check box, the card is moved to box "None".

 

To change a card

  1. Select the card in the lower part of the window.

  2. Modify it in the upper part of the window.

  3. Click Change to apply.

 

To add a new card

  1. Click New Card.

  2. Add text and media in the fields in the upper part of the window.

  3. Click Add Card.

 

To add an image, audio, or video

You can add multimedia files to the card.

 

To enter special characters

If you want to enter special characters, click the character map button .

 

To enter synonyms

You can add synonyms to your card.

 

To enter distractors

You can add distractors to your card.

 

To preview a card

If you want to preview the card you are working on, click .

 

To add or edit the Cardstyle

  1. Click the card whose style you want to modify.

  2. Click Add/Edit Cardstyle.

  3. Modify the style of the elements shown in the Question and Answer boxes of the learning window using the Cardstyle Editor.

 

Working with multiple cards

You can search for and select multiple cards, as well as deactivate, sort, delete, and assign multiple cards to a new chapter in the Maintain Cards dialog box.

 

Note:
Changes are not saved until you click Close in the Maintain Cards dialog box.
