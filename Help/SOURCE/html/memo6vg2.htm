Chapter selector

In various MemoryLifter dialog boxes, you can choose a subset from the available chapters for processing (e.g., learning).

 

The list on the left contains the available chapters, the list on the right contains the chapters you selected.

 

To add a chapter

  1. Select the chapter you want to add from the left side by clicking it (hold Ctrl while clicking to select more than one at a time).

  2. Add it either by double clicking the chapter or clicking the top arrow.

  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until all needed chapters are added.

 

To add all chapters

  1. Click the arrows.

 

To remove chapter(s)

  1. Select the chapter(s) you want to remove from the right list.

  2. Click the arrow.

To rearrange the order of the chapters

  1. Click the chapter to be moved in the right list.

  2. Drag it to the new position in the list.

  3. Release the mouse button.
