Learn - Slideshow mode

The slideshow mode allows you to go through all the cards you selected for learning. The question and answer sides are shown -  this mode is ideal for learning your cards without being assessed right or wrong at first.

 

1. On the Learn menu, select Slideshow mode.

2. Click Next to show the next cards (question and answer).

3. Click Normal Mode to switch back to normal question mode.
