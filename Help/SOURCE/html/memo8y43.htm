Synonyms

Synonyms are multiple translations for one word. MemoryLifter supports them in a unique way, unlike any other vocabulary trainers. Both sides (the question and answer) of a card can have synonyms.

 

Creating a card with synonyms

  1. Open either the Add new card screen (accessible from the Cards, Add menu) or Maintain cards screen (accessible from the Cards, Maintain menu).

  2. In the question or answer field, seperate the single synonyms by pressing Enter or Ctrl-Enter

  3. Click Add card (or change).

 

Answering a card with synonyms

  • When the cards has synonyms in the answer, you will be notified with the number of synonyms.
  • You can enter the synonyms in any order you wish.
  • In "correct on the fly" learning mode, as soon as one synonym is known, the cursor will jump into a new line, prompting you for the next one. This continues until all synonyms are known.
  • For "standard" edit learning mode, press Ctrl-Enter to advance to the next line.
  • If you want to stop after a number of synonyms, press Enter.
  • Depending on the synonym grading settings, the card is promoted or demoted.
