Recording your own audio for the cards

You can easily create your own audio file for the flashcards using MemoryLifter's built-in audio recorder.  To bring up the audio Recorder screen, click Record when adding or modifying cards.

 

 

To record audio for the card:

 

  1. Select the fields under "Recording Cue" for recording.  By default, the Question and Answer fields are selected.

  2. Click Start Cue.

  3. Click Record.

  4. Speak the word(s) you want to record. Try to make the delays between clicking and speaking as short as possible.

  5. Click Stop.

  6. Click Play if you want to hear what you have just recorded.

  7. Click Done.

 

Hints:

  • MemoryLifter will display the words from the card you are about to record under "Words to record" to help you.

  • By default, MemoryLifter will automatically assign a file name for your recorded audio.  if you want to provide a more descriptive file name after recording, click the   (...) button on the recorder screen.
