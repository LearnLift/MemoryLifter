Statistics

 

Statistics give you feedback about your learning progress. They are especially interesting over a longer time frame.

 

Note: when you Restart your learning process, statistics will be cleared.

 

To show the statistics

From the Learn menu, select Statistics… or click on the toolbar.

Use the tabs to look at the various statistics.

The time frame combo box let's you choose between 5 predefined time frames.
