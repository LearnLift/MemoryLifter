Learning in slideshow mode

Slideshow mode allows you to go through all the cards you selected for learning.

The question and answer sides are shown.

This mode is ideal for learning your cards without being assessed.

 

To learn in slideshow mode

 

  1. Within the Learn menu, click Slideshow Mode, or click on the toolbar.

  2. Click Right Arrow to show the next card (question and answer), or the Left Arrow to show the previous card.

  3. Return to normal mode by toggling Slideshow Mode in the menu or on the toolbar in the same way as Step 1.
