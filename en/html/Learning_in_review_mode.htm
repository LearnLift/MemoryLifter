Learning in self-assessment mode

MemoryLifter allows you to learn in self-assessment mode, so that you can decide whether your answer was correct or not.  

 

To learn in review mode

  1. Within the Learn menu, click Learning Options... or click on the toolbar.

  2. Click the Typing tab.

  3. Check Self Assessment.

  4. Click OK.

  5. Type your answer.

  6. Click the enter button (depending on the skin usually and "OK" button or button with a lightbulb on it).

  7. The correct answer is shown and you can click the Do Know or Don't Know button (usually "thumbs up/down", or "smile/frown" buttons next to the left/right arrow buttons) depending on whether you think you answered the card correctly or not.
    You can also click Don't ask again, if you know the card well enough already or if you do not want to learn it.
    Note:
    If you click Don't ask again, the card is moved to box "None".
    This is the same as clearing the Card is active check box in the Maintain Cards dialog box.
