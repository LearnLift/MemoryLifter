Possible uses of MemoryLifter

MemoryLifter can be used in many more applications than just vocabulary learning.

 

Here is a list of ideas for different Learning Modules:

 

Languages

  • Use example sentences to see the word you're learning in context.
    When creating your flashcard Learning Module, be sure to include an example sentence with the word you are trying to learn.

  • Sound files give you an idea of pronunciation.
    If you happen to know a native speaker, request him/her to record sounds for you.
    This process will take only a few minutes, thanks to the built-in sound recorder, and helps a lot with learning.

  • Images and videos help you to remember vocabulary.

 

Children

  • Take advantage of sounds and example sentences to introduce your child to the alphabet.

  • Memorize multiplication tables easily.

  • Teach your child the most important traffic signs and rules, utilizing image and sentence features (for rules of behavior).

 

School

  • Do you instantly recognize a country, its name, capital, and native language when just seeing the outline on a world map?
    Train your geography skills in image recognition mode, and even utilize videos to give yourself a more vivid view of the country.

  • Make the memorization of mathematical formulas a problem of the past using MemoryLifter!

  • Go a step further in physics by providing explanatory animations for the formulas you want to learn!

  • Learning important historical dates can be vastly improved by explaining the context in an example sentence or by adding illustrations.
    After having learned for some time, try the multiple-choice learning mode to see how clearly you can differentiate the dates!

  • Be it parts of the human body or evolution, flashcards make learning biology easy.

  • Do you have to learn a poem or something similar by heart?
    Use MemoryLifter for the most difficult stanzas.

 

Everyday life

  • Troubles recognizing the meaning of the multitude of abbreviations that are flooding our everyday life?
    Use the question and answer boxes for the word and its abbreviation, and the example sentence box to give you more details on the word, its context, etc.

  • Never forget birthdays, wedding anniversaries, and similar dates again.
    Yet again, use the image feature to give an even better association, or maybe add a sound file of some piece of music you connect with the respective person.

  • Let MemoryLifter help you remember names, hobbies, birthdays and other information about people you rarely see or just got to know.

  • Telephone numbers can be another application.

 

Don't forget to check the webpage for ready-made Learning Modules and further tips!
