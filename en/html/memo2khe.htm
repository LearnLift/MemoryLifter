Learning Timer

The countdown timer is a useful tool when trying to simulate a test.

For each card, the timer is set to a time depending mainly on the length of the answer as well as the number of synonyms.

 

To activate the countdown timer

  1. Within the Learn menu, click Learning Options... or click on the toolbar.

  2. Click the General tab.

  3. Check Countdown timer.

 

To deactivate the countdown timer

  1. Within the Learn menu, click Learning Options... or click .

  2. Click the General tab.

  3. Uncheck Countdown timer.
