Statistics

Viewing your learning statistics

Statistics give you feedback about your learning progress.

They are especially interesting over a long period of time.

 

Note:

When you Restart Learning, statistics will be cleared.

 

To view the statistics

  • Within the Learn menu, click Show Statistics… or click on the toolbar.

Note:

To see statistics you must have learned with and closed the Learning Module at least once.

Otherwise the message "No statistics available yet." will pop up.

 

  • Click the tabs to look at the various statistics:

    • Knowledge (Cards answered correctly over time)

    • Memory Distribution (Amount of cards in each box over time)

    • Other:

      • Time spent on the Learning Module

      • Number of cards asked

      • Number of correct answers

      • Number of wrong answers

      • Percentage of correct answers

  • In the Time frame combo box you can select five predefined time frames:

    • All

    • Today

    • Last 7 days

    • Last 31 days

    • Last 365 days

  • Click OK to close the Statistics dialog box.
