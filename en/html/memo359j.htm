Learning Progress Bar

The color and the length of the Learning Progress bar and the icon on the left of it show how well you know the cards of the current Learning Module.

 

Icon

Learning Progress Bar

Color

Status

0%  to 20%

red

You know 0% to 20% of the Learning Module.

20%  to 40%

red

You know 20% to 40% of the Learning Module.

40%  to 60%

yellow

You know 40% to 60% of the Learning Module.

60%  to 80%

green

You know 60% to 80% of the Learning Module.

80%  to 100%

green

You know 80% to 100% of the Learning Module.

 

 

In this example the Learning Progress Bar shows that you know a little more than half of the Learning Module, indicated by the yellow color of the bar and by the icon of a star filled completely with color.
