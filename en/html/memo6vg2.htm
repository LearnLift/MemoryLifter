Chapter selector

In the MemoryLifter dialog boxes Export and Chapter Selection, you can choose a subset from the available chapters for processing, i.e. for exporting or learning.

 

The list on the left contains the available chapters, the list on the right contains the chapters you've selected.

 

To add a chapter

  1. Select the chapter you want to add from the left side by clicking it.

Tip: Hold Ctrl while clicking to select more than one at a time.

  1. Add the chapter by either double-clicking it or by clicking .

  2. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until all needed chapters are added.

To remove a chapter

  1. Select the chapter you want to remove from the right side by clicking it.

Tip: Hold Ctrl while clicking to select more than one at a time.

To remove all chapters

To arrange the order of the chapters

  1. Select the chapter you want to move.

  2. Drag it to the new position in the list.
