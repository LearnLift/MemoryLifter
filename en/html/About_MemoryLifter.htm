About MemoryLifter

MemoryLifter™ is freeware educational software.

With MemoryLifter, you can

 

  • improve your vocabulary,

  • study your favorite subject,

  • or just remember various facts.

 

MemoryLifter uses Leitner's box system, a scientific learning technique proven to improve the memorization process for better retention in short-term and long-term memory.  The Leitner system combines spaced repetitions with a mechanism to focus learning on the material which is not yet mastered, this enables you to remember facts much faster and retain them longer.

 

MemoryLifter is designed for everyone, including students, parents, teachers, and professionals.  With MemoryLifter, you can easily produce your own Learning Module of virtual, multimedia flashcards. The program facilitates easy management of the flashcards and tracks your learning progress.  You can create your own flashcards with text, sound, images, and videos, or learn with ready-made expert-developed Learning Modules available in the Learning Module download section of the MemoryLifter website.

 

MemoryLifter's major features are:

 

  1. Automated Learning
    Automate your learning with Sebastian Leitner's learning system based on flashcards in boxes.
    Let the learning planner remind you when it is time to repeat the cards.

  2. Multimedia-based Learning
    Enhance your learning experience by including media like images, audio, and video in your Learning Modules.

  3. Individual Learning
    Create your own Learning Modules for specific topics with the help of MemoryLifter's import and editing features.
    Design individual cards by defining your own styles.
    Choose your favorite Learning Mode - standard text input, multiple choice with random or predefined distractors, listening comprehension, self assessment, or slideshow mode.

  4. Measure Your Success
    Get statistical feedback to help you understand how you learn, and improve your learning efficiency.
