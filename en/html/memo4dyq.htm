Sound recorder

Recording your own audio for cards

The built-in sound recorder is a quick and useful tool for recording sounds to use in your Learning Module.

 

It is accessible from

 

  • the Maintain Cards dialog box

  • the Advanced tab on the Learning Module Options dialog box
     

by clicking either the Audio button or .

 

 

To record a sound
 

  1. Click the Audio button or to open the sound recorder.

  2. Click Record to start recording.

  3. Say the word or sentence.

  4. Click Stop to stop recording.

  5. If desired, click Play to test the sound.

  6. Click Close when satisfied.

 

Notes:

  • Try to keep the delays between the start of the recording and the speaking, as well as the end of the recording, as short as possible.

  • Depending on what you are recording, some options of the sound recorder will be enabled or disabled.

  • By default, MemoryLifter will automatically assign a file name to your recorded audio.
