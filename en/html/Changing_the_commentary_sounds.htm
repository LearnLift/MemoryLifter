Learning with commentary sounds

MemoryLifter uses commentary sounds to help encourage the user.
You can disable or change the commentary sounds if you like.

 

To change the commentary sounds:
 

Follow the instruction in Properties.

 

To disable the commentary sounds:

  1. Within the Learn menu, click Learning Options... or click on the toolbar.

  2. Click the General tab.

  3. Uncheck Play commentary sound.
