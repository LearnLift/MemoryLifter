Edit Chapters

Using chapters to organize cards

Chapters are used to organize your cards.
For instance, you can create a chapter to hold all cards about 'Dining out', and another chapter for cards about 'Shopping'.  

The Manage Chapters dialog box allows you to add, modify, and delete chapters and their descriptions.

 

To open the Manage Chapters dialog box

Within the Edit menu, click Edit Chapters...

 

To add a new chapter

  1. Click New.

Note:

MemoryLifter creates a default chapter name for you.

 

  1. Type the chapter information in the Title and Description boxes as needed, and click Apply.

  2. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until done.

  3. Click OK to close the Manage Chapters box.

 

To delete a chapter

  1. Click the chapter you want to delete.

  2. Click Delete.

Caution:
This cannot be undone!

 

To change the order of the chapters

  1. Click the chapter you want to move.

  2. Drag the chapter to the new place in the list.

Note:

This allows you to re-arrange the chapters if you did not have a specific order when you created them.

 

To modify chapter title and description

  1. Click the chapter you want to modify.

  2. Type the new chapter information in the Title and Description boxes.

  3. Click Apply.

 

To add or edit the chapter style

  1. Click the chapter whose style you want to modify.

  2. Click Add/Edit Style.

  3. Modify the style of the elements shown in the Question and Answer boxes of the learning window using the Cardstyle Editor.
