Switch Skin

You can change the way MemoryLifter's learning window looks! You can choose between five pre-defined styles:

 

  • Default

  • BumbleBee

  • iLifter

  • Kids

  • TropicalBeach

 

To change the skin

  • Within the Tools menu, click Switch Skin, then click the name of the skin you want to use.

Note:

The selected skin is marked with a checkmark.
