Character Map

With the character map, you can enter some special or foreign characters that you need for your cards but are not on your keyboard.

 

To use the character map

  1. Within the Tools menu, click Character Map or click on the toolbar.
    Note:     The button will be pressed down, when selected.

  2. Click the character in symbol box that you wish to add.

 

To stop using the character map

  • Within the Tools menu, click Character Map or click on the toolbar.
    Note:     The button will be up, when unselected.
