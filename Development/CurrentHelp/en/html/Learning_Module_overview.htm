Learning Module overview

 

To restrict shown cards

Select either "All cards" or a specific chapter from the Show chapter combo box.

 

To sort shown cards

You can sort the cards displayed in ascending or descending order by clicking on the appropriate column headings.  

For example, to display the cards based on the boxes they are in, click the "Box" column heading.

 

To select cards

  1. To select a single card, click the desired card in the lower part of the window.

Note: The content is displayed in the upper part of the window.

 

  1. To select multiple cards, use the standard Windows Shift-click or Ctrl-click method.
     

To delete cards

  1. Select one or more card(s).

  2. Click Delete Card(s) or right-click and select Delete.

Caution:
This action cannot be undone!

 

To move cards to a certain chapter

  1. Select one or more card(s).

  2. Right-click and click Move to, then click Chapter, then click the chapter which you wish to move the card to.

 

To move cards to a certain box

  1. Select one or more cards.

  2. Right-click and click Move to, then click Box, then click the box you want to move the card(s) to.

 

To search for cards

  1. Click in the Type to search... field

  2. Type a keyword.
    Note:         The cards matching this keyword are shown.
