Restarting a learning session

You can restart your current learning session and empty all boxes, thus resetting your learning progress.

 

To restart a learning session:

  1. Within the Learn menu, click Restart Learning.

  2. MemoryLifter asks you if you really want to empty all boxes – click Yes.

  3. If prompted, in the Chapter Selection dialog box, select the chapters to learn with the chapter selector.

  4. Click OK.
