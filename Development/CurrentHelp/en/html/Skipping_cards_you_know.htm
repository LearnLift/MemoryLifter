Skipping cards you already know

You can tell MemoryLifter to stop asking cards you already know.

 

To skip asking the current card

  1. Within the Edit menu, click Maintain Cards... or click on the toolbar.

  2. Uncheck the Card is active box.

 

To skip asking a specific card

  1. Within the Edit menu, click Maintain Cards... or click on the toolbar.

  2. Select the desired card in the Learning Module overview.

  3. Uncheck the Card is active box.
