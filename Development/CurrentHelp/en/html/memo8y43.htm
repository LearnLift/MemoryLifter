Synonyms

Using synonyms in question and answer

A synonym is a word or a phrase with the same meaning as another in the same language.

 

MemoryLifter supports multiple translations for one word in each direction, unlike other vocabulary trainers.

Both sides (the question and answer) of a card can have synonyms.

 

If you create Learning Modules other than vocabulary Learning Modules, you can use the synonym feature for optional correct answers.

 

To create a card with synonyms or add synonyms to a card

  1. Open the Maintain Cards dialog box by clicking either Add Card... or Maintain Cards... in the Edit menu or by clicking [ADD CARD ICON] on the toolbar.

  2. In the question or answer field, separate the single synonyms by pressing Enter or Ctrl-Enter
    or by clicking and selecting the next empty row.

  3. Click Add Card (or Save Changes).

 

 

To answer a card with synonyms

  • When a card has synonyms in the answer, you will be told the number of synonyms in parentheses next to the answer pane header.

  • You can enter the synonyms in any order you wish.

  • In "correct on the fly" learning mode, the cursor will jump to the next line as soon as a synonym is correctly entered.
    You are prompted to enter the next synonym until all synonyms are known.

  • For "standard" edit learning mode, press Enter to advance to the next line.

  • After entering each of the synonyms press ENTER again to submit your answer.

  • Depending on the synonym grading settings, the card will be promoted or demoted.
