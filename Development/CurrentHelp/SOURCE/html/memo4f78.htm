Print

Printing cards

This feature lets you print out a list of the words in your dictionary.

 

To print:

  1. On the File menu, select Print…click on the toolbar.

  2. Select the chapters you want to print in the chapter selector. By default, all the chapters are selected.

  3. If you want multimedia contents to be printed as well (e.g. images) check the "Multimedia Content" box.

  4. Click Preview to open a window with a preview of the printed pages

  5. Click Print... to start printing.
