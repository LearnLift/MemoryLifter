Manage chapters

Using chapters to organize cards into logical topics

Chapters are used to organize your vocabularies (cards) into logical topics. For instance, you can create a chapter to hold all cards about 'Dining out', and another chapter for cards about 'shopping'.  The 'Manage Chapter' window allows you to add, modify, and delete chapter information.

 

Opening the Manage Chapter dialog box

From the Cards menu, select Manage Chapters.

 

Adding a new chapter

  1. Click New. Note: MemoryLifter creates a default chapter name for you.

  2. Modify the chapter information in the Title: and Description: fields as needed, and click Apply.

  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until done.

  4. Click OK to close the Manage Chapter window.

 

Modifying chapter title and description

  1. Select a chapter title from the list.

  2. Modify the chapter information in the Title: and Description: fields.

  3. Click Apply.

  4. Click OK to close the Manage Chapter window.

 

Deleting a chapter

  1. Select a chapter title from the list.

  2. Click Delete. Warning: This cannot be undone!

 

Changing the order of the chapter

  1. Click on the chapter you want to move.

  2. Click and hold the left mouse button down.

  3. Drag item to new place in the list.

  4. Release the mouse button.

Note: This allows you to re-arrange the chapters if you did not have a specific order when you created them.
