Packing a dictionary

Use this option to pack the currently opened dictionary into a compressed dictionary for easy distribution of dictionaries to friends, colleagues, and others.  The compressed dictionary is stored with a .DZP (Dictionary Zipped) extension.

 

To pack:

  1. Select Pack.. on the File menu or click on the toolbar.

  2. Choose the directory where you want your dictionary to be saved and enter a name.

  3. Click Save.

 

Hint: After the dictionary is packed, you can give the file to your friends or colleagues for them to use.
