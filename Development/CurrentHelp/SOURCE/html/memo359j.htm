Scoring

The score gives you an idea on how good you currently grade in the learning process. The higher the box of a correctly card answered is the more points it will give.

If you activated the timer for learning your answer time also will affect the score you receive for a correct answer. Quick answers will give you a higher score.

 

If you give a wrong answer for a card and it falls back into box one, your score will not be decreased. Therefore your score will always increase.
